Welcome to Chrome Ridge Bengals

What started as a love for these magnificent spotted cats has grown into a dedicated breeding program focused on health, temperament, and stunning beauty.

At Chrome Ridge, we believe every Bengal deserves a loving home, and every family deserves a healthy, well-socialized companion. Our cats are raised underfoot in our home, surrounded by love and attention from day one.

We are committed to the Bengal breed standard, working with champion bloodlines to produce kittens with exceptional rosettes, glittered coats, and that signature wild look paired with a sweet, playful personality.
